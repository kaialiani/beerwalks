BeerWalks

BeerWalks are outdoor experiences with beer. It is guided city walks with stories and beer tasting at a pace and language where everyone can join. There is room for everyone on our guided walks with beer and stories, as long as you are over 18 years old. We do not nerd with the historical aspects of the city, nor with the beer, the only thing we nerd with is the overall experience on a BeerWalk. We will be happy if you have had a few enjoyable hours with us and other curious beer-tasting city walkers. The guests say that a BeerWalk is "a nice respite in a busy day" - and that is exactly what we want to help deliver. We are proud to offer BeerWalk, in collaboration with leading craft beer breweries, in cities we ourselves love to be in and explore. BeerWalks can be tailored as private BeerWalks and as corporate BeerWalks.
