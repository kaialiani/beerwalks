BeerTalks

Competence development for staff at bars, restaurants, hotels and the like where there is good beer on tap.

BeerTalks is a value proposition - a Beer School - which targets bars, restaurants, hotels or the like, which have in common that there is good beer on tap but the knowledge and competence of the employees who serve the beer lags - we want to help! Flat beer is a bad experience and so is it if you as a customer seek knowledge about the beer on tap, but are met with… nothing… NOBODY wants to not provide proper, good and competent service to their customers, it is not a good feeling not to be able to answer customers' relevant questions about beer properly. We can and would like to help with that.
