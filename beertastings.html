BeerTastings

Become a hero, arrange a BeerTasting privately, or a company BeerTasting

We guarantee that it will be some enjoyable hours that are not for beer geeks, on the contrary, we want to make beer available to everyone - so here everyone can join. We combine beer tasting with storytelling and quizzes. Of course we tell about brewing method and of course you have to taste a lot of different beer styles, but we put more emphasis on what you taste and stories about the breweries and the beer, like wrapping for a few hours. This heavenly drink, which often has only four ingredients, is HOT and interest is rising. Get on the beer cart and stand out the next time you go to the bar with your friends, by slinging around with expressions and most importantly, maybe you can guide your friends in the direction of the beer that suits them exactly! We come out to you and have it all, glasses, beer, rinsing bowls, etc. - you just have to make room for your guests and or let us know about the entertainment!
